About Us

Established in 2017, Gurugram University is a dynamic and vibrant institution located in the heart of Gurugram, Haryana. Our urban campus serves as a hub of academic and professional excellence with a global vision. We nurture a dynamic environment where students from diverse backgrounds can learn, grow, and transform into the leaders and innovators of tomorrow. Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated faculty ensure that our students receive a holistic education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical application.


Why the Hackathon

Unleash your innovation at Gurugram University's Hackathon! This exciting event provides you with the opportunity to collaborate with talented peers, brainstorm creative solutions, and bring your ideas to life in just a few days. Participants develop valuable technical skills, build a strong portfolio, and network with industry professionals. The Hackathon is not just a competition; it's an experiential learning event that pushes your limits, fosters teamwork, and enhances problem-solving skills. It's the perfect platform to turn your potential into reality while having fun.
